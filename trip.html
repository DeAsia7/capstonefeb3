Background Information

K-pop is korean pop music from Seoul, the heart of the kpop industry, South Korea. Gangnam district serving as the "birthplace" of the genre. The culture started in the 1990s and has grew ever since. They focus on pop music but theres other genres that hides in the background.

Here are cities to visit in the heart of Seoul

  • Apgujeong
  • Myeongdong
  • Cheongdam
  • Busan
  • Jeju Island

Things to do in South Korea

  • K-pop dance classes: Learn choreography from your favorite K-pop songs.
  • Fan club events: Check for fan club gatherings or special events hosted by K-pop agencies.
  • K-pop related tours: Join guided tours focused on K-pop landmarks and filming locations.
  • Busan K-pop attractions: Visit Busan, another major city with its own K-pop scene.
  • Jeju Island K-pop experiences: Explore Jeju Island where some K-pop groups have filmed music videos.
  • K star road in apgujeong
  • shopping district in Myeongdong

K-pop related tours

There are several K-pop tours you can take to get a better experience. they include walking tours, theme park visits, and tours of fliming locations.

Walking tours

Walking tours are a great way to see the city and learn about the history of K-pop. You can visit the Gangnam district, where many K-pop agencies are located, and see the famous K star road in apgujeong.

Theme park visits

Theme parks are a popular destination for K-pop fans. You can visit Everland, the largest theme park in South Korea, and enjoy rides, shows, and attractions.

SM TOWN: Experience the life of an artist in the vocal and dance training room, recording room, and music video studio

Tours of filming locations

Many K-pop music videos are filmed in South Korea. You can take a tour of filming locations and see where your favorite music videos were made.

Visit the filming locations of popular K-pop music videos, such as Gangnam Style by Psy, and see where your favorite artists have performed.

Visit the filming locations of popular K-pop dramas and see where your favorite actors have filmed.

Visit the filming locations of popular K-pop variety shows and see where your favorite idols have appeared.

Visit the filming locations of popular K-pop reality shows and see where your favorite idols have lived.

Visit the filming locations of popular K-pop concerts and see where your favorite artists have performed.
